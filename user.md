No cloud, no tracking, just secure local intelligence.

## ⚡ Quick Start (Cheat Sheet)  

- **Model File**: Place `qwen2.5-0.5b-instruct-q4_0.gguf` in the app’s storage folder.  
- **Ready Signal**: Progress bar → **green dot** = AI is ready.  
- **Send Message**: Type + tap **Send (arrow)**.  
- **Voice Input**: Tap **Microphone** (English & Japanese).  
- **Stop Response**: Tap **Stop button** if output is too long.  
- **Retry Answer**: Tap **Refresh icon** under a message bubble.  
- **Text‑to‑Speech**: Tap **Speaker icon** to hear replies.  
- **Settings**: Gear icon → Theme, Dark Mode, Persona.  

---

## 🛠️ Getting Started  

When you first open RakuGEN, the AI model needs to load into your phone’s memory.  

- **Model File**: Place `your model.gguf` in the app’s storage folder ensure that model is in .gguf format .  
- **Loading**: A progress bar will appear at the top. Once it finishes, you’ll see a **green dot** — that means RakuGEN is ready.  

---

## 💬 Chatting with RakuGEN  

- **Send Messages**: Type your question and tap the **Send (arrow)** button.  
- **Voice Input**: Tap the **Microphone** to speak in English or Japanese.  
- **Stop Response**: If RakuGEN is writing too much, the Microphone turns into a **Stop button**. Tap it to halt instantly.  
- **Retry**: Not satisfied with an answer? Tap the **Refresh icon** under the message bubble to generate a new response.  

---

## 🔊 Voice & Language Tools  

RakuGEN is designed with language learning in mind, especially Japanese.  

- **Text‑to‑Speech**: Tap the **Speaker icon** to hear any AI response read aloud.  
- **Furigana Support**: Enable this in Settings to display reading aids above Kanji characters.  

---

## 🎨 Personalization  

Access **Settings** via the **Gear icon** (top right).  

- **Theme Color**: Choose from multiple color options.  
- **Dark Mode**: Switch between Light and Dark for comfort.  
- **Custom Persona**: Enter a role or character style for the AI to adopt.  

---

## 🧠 System Diagnostics  

For advanced users, go to **Settings → System Diagnostics → Hardware Manifest**.  

Here you’ll find:  
- **RAM Usage**: Current memory load.  
- **Processor (SOC)**: Your phone’s chipset details.  
- **Thermal Envelope**: Real‑time device temperature (Green = normal, Red = overheating).  

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Safety  

- **Offline First**: All chats stay on your device. Nothing is sent online.  
- **Accuracy Note**: AI can make mistakes. Double‑check important translations or factual details.  

---

## 📬 Support & Feedback  

Found a bug or have suggestions? Go to **Settings → Send Feedback**.  

- The app will attach technical details automatically to help with troubleshooting.  
- Your email app will open so you can send feedback directly.
